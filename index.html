Three's a Fever

Sophia was always the life of the party, but tonight something felt different. Gauge noticed her unease and decided to check in. "Are you okay?" he asked gently. Sophia hesitated, then admitted, "I think I’ve got a fever." Before they could react, Justin barged in, causing a stir. The night spiraled into chaos — a fever, unexpected guests, and secrets unraveling. In the end, the trio learned that sometimes the wildest moments make the best stories.
